Aon Pulse Survey - Roth Catch-Up Contributions for High Earners

In the summer of 2025, clients started reaching out concerned about what they were hearing or, in many cases not hearing, from their providers about implementing SECURE 2.0's Roth requirement for high earners. As a result of industry concern, I proposed facilitating a survey so that employers could see how others were tackling the challenging implementation.
